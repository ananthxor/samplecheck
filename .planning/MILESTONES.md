# Milestones: ScrollToday

## v1.0 — Core Platform (Completed 2026-02-24)

**Goal:** Ship a functional self-service ad platform — template-based creative builder, ad serving infrastructure, billing, and analytics — in one milestone.

**Phases completed:** 1–10 (25 plans)

| Phase | Name | Plans | Completed |
|-------|------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Foundation & Data Schema | 2 | 2026-02-19 |
| 2 | Authentication & Admin | 3 | 2026-02-19 |
| 3 | Dashboard & Navigation Shell | 2 | 2026-02-20 |
| 4 | Template Library & Ad Editor | 3 | 2026-02-20 |
| 5 | Interactive Ad Formats | 2 | 2026-02-20 |
| 6 | Animated, Video, Standard & Native Formats | 3 | 2026-02-23 |
| 7 | Campaign Management & Tag Export | 3 | 2026-02-23 |
| 8 | Ad Serving Infrastructure | 4 | 2026-02-24 |
| 9 | Billing & Credit System | 3 | 2026-02-24 |
| 10 | Analytics & Reporting | 2 | 2026-02-24 |

**What shipped:**
- 20 curated templates across 14 ad formats (7 interactive, 4 animated/video, 3 standard/native)
- Template-based ad editor with live preview, save-to-library, shareable preview links
- Invite-only auth with super admin user management
- Campaign management with ad lifecycle, creative assignment, tag export, third-party trackers
- Self-hosted ad serving with impression/click/viewability tracking and credit gate
- Stripe prepaid credit packs (50k/200k/1M impressions) with balance display and low-balance warnings
- Analytics dashboard with KPI cards, time-series charts, daily rollup pipeline, CSV export

**Last phase number:** 10
